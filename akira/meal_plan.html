Meal breakdown

Monday, 2026-07-20

Lunch — Lentil Feta Farro Salad (20g protein)

Farro, lentils, roasted peppers, feta, and arugula with red-wine vinaigrette.

Portable: yes · Microwave friendly: no · Prep: 24 minutes

Ingredients:

  • arugula (5 oz)
  • roasted red peppers (1 jar)
  • shallot (1)
  • feta (5 oz)
  • farro (1 cup dry)
  • cooked lentils (2 cups)
  • olive oil (3 tbsp)
  • red wine vinegar (2 tbsp)

Prep note: Pack arugula on top so it stays fresh until lunch.

Dinner — Harissa Chickpea Halloumi Tray Bake (20g protein)

A sheet-pan dinner with chickpeas, carrots, red onion, and halloumi under a mild harissa glaze.

One-pan preferred: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • carrots (4)
  • red onion (1)
  • parsley (1 bunch)
  • halloumi (8 oz)
  • chickpeas (2 cans)
  • harissa (2 tbsp)
  • olive oil (2 tbsp)

Prep note: Use a mild harissa so the leftovers stay approachable and not too intense.

Tuesday, 2026-07-21

Lunch — Edamame Brown Rice Crunch Boxes (19g protein)

Brown rice, edamame, cucumbers, peanuts, and sesame dressing in bento-style boxes.

Portable: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 18 minutes

Ingredients:

  • cucumber (1)
  • radishes (1 bunch)
  • scallions (1 bunch)
  • shelled edamame (16 oz)
  • brown rice (1 cup dry)
  • peanuts (3/4 cup)
  • sesame oil (1 tbsp)
  • soy sauce (2 tbsp)
  • rice vinegar (2 tbsp)

Prep note: These are fine cold but also reheat well if you want the rice warm.

Dinner — Sesame Egg Fried Rice with Edamame (19g protein)

A fast skillet dinner with eggs, edamame, rice, and plenty of crunchy vegetables.

One-pan preferred: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • carrots (2)
  • scallions (1 bunch)
  • eggs (6)
  • shelled edamame (12 oz)
  • cooked rice (4 cups)
  • sesame oil (1 tbsp)
  • soy sauce (3 tbsp)

Prep note: Great for using leftover rice and any extra scallions or carrots from lunches.

Wednesday, 2026-07-22

Lunch — Black Bean Greek Yogurt Wraps (18g protein)

Whole-wheat wraps filled with black beans, corn, pepper jack, and a Greek yogurt spread.

Portable: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 16 minutes

Ingredients:

  • romaine (1 head)
  • corn kernels (1.5 cups)
  • bell pepper (1)
  • Greek yogurt (3/4 cup)
  • pepper jack cheese (5 oz)
  • black beans (2 cans)
  • whole-wheat tortillas (4 large)

Prep note: Wrap tightly in foil so they hold up in a work bag and reheat cleanly.

Dinner — Black Bean Sweet Potato Skillet with Eggs (18g protein)

Sweet potatoes, black beans, greens, and baked eggs in one sturdy skillet dinner.

One-pan preferred: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 28 minutes

Ingredients:

  • sweet potatoes (2 medium)
  • kale (1 bunch)
  • yellow onion (1)
  • eggs (4)
  • cheddar (4 oz)
  • black beans (2 cans)
  • smoked paprika (1 tsp)
  • olive oil (2 tbsp)

Prep note: Dice the sweet potato small so the total cook time stays under half an hour.

Thursday, 2026-07-23

Lunch — Greek Yogurt Chickpea Salad Pitas (28g protein)

Chickpeas, chopped vegetables, feta, and Greek yogurt packed separately with whole-wheat pita.

Portable: yes · Microwave friendly: no · Prep: 18 minutes

Ingredients:

  • cucumber (1)
  • grape tomatoes (1 pint)
  • red onion (1)
  • parsley (1 bunch)
  • Greek yogurt (1 cup)
  • feta (4 oz)
  • chickpeas (2 cans)
  • olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • lemon (1)
  • whole-wheat pita (4 rounds)

Prep note: Mix the filling on Sunday night; pack the pita separately so it stays dry.

Dinner — One-Skillet Tofu Teriyaki Rice (24g protein)

Crisp tofu, broccoli, and rice finished in a quick teriyaki-style glaze.

One-pan preferred: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 28 minutes

Ingredients:

  • broccoli (1 large head)
  • scallions (1 bunch)
  • ginger (1 knob)
  • extra-firm tofu (14 oz)
  • cooked rice (4 cups)
  • soy sauce (3 tbsp)
  • maple syrup (1 tbsp)
  • cornstarch (1 tbsp)

Prep note: Use leftover rice to keep this firmly in the easy-weeknight bucket.

Friday, 2026-07-24

Lunch — Cottage Cheese Quinoa Crunch Bowls (24g protein)

Cold quinoa bowls with cottage cheese, almonds, edamame, and crisp vegetables.

Portable: yes · Microwave friendly: no · Prep: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • carrots (3)
  • snap peas (8 oz)
  • scallions (1 bunch)
  • cottage cheese (2 cups)
  • shelled edamame (12 oz)
  • quinoa (1 cup dry)
  • almonds (1 cup)
  • soy sauce (2 tbsp)
  • rice vinegar (2 tbsp)

Prep note: Keep almonds separate until lunch for better texture.

Dinner — Paneer Pepper Fajita Skillet (23g protein)

Paneer, onions, and peppers tossed with fajita spices and served with warm tortillas.

One-pan preferred: yes · Microwave friendly: yes · Prep: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • bell peppers (3)
  • yellow onion (1 large)
  • lime (1)
  • paneer (10 oz)
  • whole-wheat tortillas (6)
  • olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • fajita seasoning (1 tbsp)

Prep note: If you want leftovers, slice the paneer thick so it reheats without drying out.

Sunday prep / shared components

  • Shared ingredients to batch once: feta, olive oil, cucumber, scallions, shelled edamame, sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar.
  • Cook one base grain on Sunday if the plan includes quinoa, farro, couscous, or rice so lunches assemble quickly.
  • Wash and chop crunchy vegetables in advance; keep dressings and nuts separate for work lunches.
  • Press tofu, roast vegetables, and boil eggs in the same prep block to keep the weeknight dinners easy.

Rotation notes

  • Lunches remain portable and low-mess for work; dinners stay weeknight-easy.
