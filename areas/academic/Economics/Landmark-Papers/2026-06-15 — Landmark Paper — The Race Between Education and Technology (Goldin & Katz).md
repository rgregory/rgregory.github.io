# The Race Between Education and Technology

**Authors**: Claudia Goldin, Lawrence F. Katz  
**Year**: 2008  
**Citations**: ~4,300+ (cited heavily 2016–2026)  
**Venue**: Belknap Press

## Key Contribution

Traces 130+ years of education-technology competition in US labor markets. Hypothesis: inequality driven by whether educational supply keeps pace with skill-biased technological demand. Pre-1980: education outpaced tech (inequality fell). Post-1980: tech outpaced education (inequality rose). Long-run education investment critical for shared prosperity.

## Intellectual Impact

Foundational for understanding long-run inequality dynamics. Widely cited in education, labor, growth, and inequality literatures. Shaped policy arguments for education investment as inequality remedy.
