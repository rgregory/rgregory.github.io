What we learned

LearningEvidenceConsequence
Exit codes are too crude for Akira’s mature workflows.Car-search artifact existed while the scheduler still carried an earlier failed state.Keep phase evidence: acquire, persist, publish, notify, schedule.
Binary/raw provenance should not become fake graph nodes.Menu and ODU sources were HTML/PNG artifacts, not Markdown notes.Use source_files and Markdown links for raw files; graph only durable concepts/entities.
Graph correctness is semantic, not numerical.Current graph still has 88 unresolved and 50 ambiguous wikilinks.Prioritize high-reference ambiguous links before chasing raw count reduction.
Primary sources often validate less than the family narrative contains.ODU confirms Wheeler/Gregory founded the fellowship in 1975; godfather/deceased/pre-ODU context is family-stated.Preserve both, but label evidence status explicitly.

The executive read

Akira is crossing from a personal assistant that can do tasks into a source-governed operating memory: it now captures primary evidence, turns it into graphable notes, records workflow health by phase, and exposes what remains uncertain. The next improvement is not more automation. It is better review pressure on the graph and on action boundaries.
