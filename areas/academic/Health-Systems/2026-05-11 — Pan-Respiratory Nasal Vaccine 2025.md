# Pan-Respiratory Nasal Vaccine (2025)

## Discovery
Single nasal vaccine provides protection against multiple respiratory illnesses including COVID-19 and influenza.

## Coverage
- COVID-19
- Influenza (flu)
- Other respiratory illnesses (RSV, etc.)

## Innovation: Nasal Route
- Mucosal immunity at site of infection
- Non-invasive delivery
- Potentially stronger local immune response than intramuscular injection

## Advantages
- Consolidates multiple vaccines into one dose
- Improves compliance (one needle vs. many)
- Triggers both systemic and mucosal immunity
- May provide broader cross-protection

## Clinical Significance
Shifts paradigm from disease-specific vaccines to broad respiratory protection, simplifying vaccination schedules and improving population immunity.
