# Kordofan and Darfur

> Stub — to be expanded. Focus: history of these regions and what led to current events in 2026.

## Background

Two regions in Sudan (and South Sudan border zones):
- **Kordofan**: central Sudan, divided into North, South, and West Kordofan states. Long history of conflict between Arab pastoralists and Nuba farming communities.
- **Darfur**: western Sudan. Site of a devastating conflict beginning in 2003, described by some as genocide. Involves government-backed Arab Janjaweed militia vs. non-Arab Fur, Masalit, and Zaghawa peoples.
