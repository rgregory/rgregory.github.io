# RSV Vaccine (2023)

## Discovery
Phase 3 randomized trials demonstrated RSV vaccines reduce infant hospitalization by **80–90%**.

## Target Population
Infants vulnerable to severe respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) infection, which previously had no vaccine.

## Clinical Efficacy
- 80–90% reduction in hospitalization rates
- FDA approval in mid-2023
- Addresses major gap in pediatric vaccine coverage

## Disease Context
RSV is a leading cause of lower respiratory tract infection in infants and young children globally. No approved vaccine existed for decades despite high disease burden.

## Public Health Impact
- Prevents severe bronchiolitis and pneumonia
- Reduces healthcare burden
- Enables early protection before peak RSV season
