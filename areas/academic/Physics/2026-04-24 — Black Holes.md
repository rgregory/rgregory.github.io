## Connections

- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Planck Length|Planck Length]] — the Bekenstein-Hawking entropy formula ($S = k_B A / 4\ell_P^2$) measures black hole entropy in Planck areas; the Compton-Schwarzschild argument shows that probing below the Planck length creates a micro black hole that destroys the measurement; Hawking radiation wavelength equals the Planck length for a Planck-mass black hole
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Holographic Principle|Holographic Principle]] — black holes are the origin of holography: Bekenstein's entropy bound, Hawking radiation, and the information paradox together forced the conclusion that information is stored on surfaces, not volumes; black holes saturate the Bekenstein bound and are therefore the maximally holographic objects in nature
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Primordial Black Holes|Primordial Black Holes]] — cosmological complement; PBHs form from density fluctuations before any stars existed; they extend the black hole concept to masses from the Planck mass up to thousands of solar masses; evaporating PBHs are the only black holes where Hawking radiation may be observable
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Baryogenesis|Baryogenesis]] — Hawking radiation is the physical basis for PBH baryogenesis: as a black hole evaporates, its temperature ($T_H = \hbar c^3 / 8\pi G M k_B$) climbs until GUT-scale baryon-number-violating decays occur; the irreversible, non-equilibrium character of evaporation satisfies Sakharov condition 3; black hole thermodynamics is thus directly relevant to why anything exists at all
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Schwarzschild vs Kerr Black Holes|Schwarzschild vs Kerr Black Holes]] — the two fundamental exact solutions that define all astrophysical black holes; Schwarzschild (non-rotating, one horizon, point singularity) vs. Kerr (rotating, two horizons, ring singularity, ergosphere, Penrose process); the no-hair theorem and the observational evidence from M87* are treated here in full

- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Quantum Mechanics|Quantum Mechanics]] — Hawking radiation is a quantum effect in curved spacetime: virtual particle pairs at the event horizon are separated by tidal forces, with one partner escaping as real radiation; the black hole information paradox arises because Hawking radiation appears thermal, threatening QM's unitarity
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — General Relativity|General Relativity]] — GR is the theory that predicts black holes: the Schwarzschild solution, singularity theorems (Penrose 1965), and event horizons are all GR predictions; the singularity at $r = 0$ is where GR breaks down and a quantum theory of gravity is required
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — String Theory|String Theory]] — Strominger and Vafa (1996) used D-brane counting to derive the Bekenstein-Hawking entropy microscopically for extremal black holes; the first quantitative microscopic success of string theory
- [[02-Areas/Learning/Self-Study/Physics/2026-05-15 — Loop Quantum Gravity|Loop Quantum Gravity]] — LQG derives Bekenstein-Hawking entropy via spin network puncture counting on the horizon; predicts Planck stars replacing the interior singularity via the quantum bounce mechanism
