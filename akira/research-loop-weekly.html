Executive read

The portfolio is bifurcated. Several loops now contain substantive, decision-oriented findings; older biology, mathematics, statistics, and architecture loops remain source-queue heavy. Across domains, the strongest shared signal is a demand for inspectable evidence, explicit boundaries, and disciplined separation of observation, inference, and decision.
