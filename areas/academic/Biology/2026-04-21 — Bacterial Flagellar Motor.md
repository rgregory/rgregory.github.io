# Bacterial Flagellar Motor

Nanoscale rotary motor that propels bacterial movement. A striking example of [[biology]] at the molecular scale — demonstrates how simple chemical gradients produce directed motion through rotary mechanisms.

![Flagellar motor structure](https://www.quantamagazine.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Flagellar-motor-graphic-MOBILE.svg)
