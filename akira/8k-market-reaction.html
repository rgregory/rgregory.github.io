Sources & caveats. Companies from board-cybersecurity.com's incident tracker (source "SEC 8-K"), enriched with SEC EDGAR filing metadata (item numbers, acceptance timestamps). Item 1.05 = material incident disclosure; other items (e.g. 8.01) are voluntary. Daily dividend- and split-adjusted closes from Yahoo Finance (Stooq fallback is unadjusted). "vs SPY" columns subtract SPY's return over the identical window. 7 of 50 companies had no retrievable price history — often delisted or restructured names, so surviving-company medians likely overstate how well filers fared. ⚠︎ marks companies with an earnings filing inside the one-month window (return confounded). Amendments are deduplicated to one row per company. Incidents often become public before the 8-K, so part of the reaction can predate day 0. A small, recent sample — treat any pattern as suggestive, not statistical proof.
