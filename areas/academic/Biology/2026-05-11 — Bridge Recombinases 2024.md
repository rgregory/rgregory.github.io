# Bridge Recombinases (2024)

## Discovery
Arc Institute researchers discovered a new mechanism called **IS622** that enables editing of nearly 1 million base pairs in human cells.

## Key Advantage Over CRISPR
Bridge recombinases target genomic regions far beyond CRISPR's reach:
- Entire genes
- Long regulatory elements
- Clustered gene families

## Mechanism
Recombinases work through a different molecular pathway than CRISPR/Cas9, using site-specific recombination to excise or rearrange large DNA segments.

## Clinical Promise
Opens therapeutic possibilities for:
- Multi-gene disorders
- Complex regulatory mutations
- Structural genomic rearrangements
