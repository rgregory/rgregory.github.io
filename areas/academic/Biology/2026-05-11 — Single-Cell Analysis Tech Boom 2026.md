# Single-Cell Analysis Tech Boom (2026)

## Discovery
Single-cell analysis technologies advancing rapidly, now being applied across clinical and research domains.

## Technologies
- **scRNA-seq:** single-cell RNA sequencing
- **scATAC-seq:** single-cell chromatin accessibility
- **Spatial transcriptomics:** location + gene expression
- **Protein analysis:** single-cell proteomics
- **Multi-omics:** simultaneous measurement of multiple molecular layers

## Clinical Applications (2026)

### Early Disease Detection
- Identify disease states before symptoms
- Detect cancer cells in circulation (liquid biopsy)
- Monitor minimal residual disease

### Prenatal Screening
- Non-invasive fetal cell analysis
- Genetic abnormality detection
- Earlier diagnostic confirmation than traditional methods

### Biomarker Discovery
- Patient-specific biomarkers for disease stratification
- Predictive markers for drug response
- Therapeutic target identification

### Liquid Biopsies
- Blood-based cell and molecular analysis
- Circulating tumor cells (CTCs)
- Cell-free DNA and RNA
- Monitoring treatment response

### Biologic Drug Development
- Characterize cellular response to therapeutics
- Quality control and batch release testing
- Patient-specific manufacturing (CAR-T cells, etc.)

## Significance
Single-cell resolution reveals:
- Cellular heterogeneity within tissues
- Rare cell populations driving disease
- Tumor/immune microenvironment composition
- True disease mechanisms obscured by bulk analysis
