# The Long and Short of Cryptocurrency

**Author**: Kenneth Rogoff  
**Year**: 2017+  
**Citations**: ~1,100+  
**Venue**: Multiple papers and essays

## Key Contribution

Rogoff's sustained analysis of cryptocurrency: challenges bitcoin's use as medium of exchange and store of value; contextualizes within history of currency competition and speculative bubbles. Skeptical of decentralization benefits while acknowledging blockchain innovation.

## Intellectual Impact

Influential in finance and policy circles. Cross-domain: cited in monetary economics, regulatory debates, technology criticism. Counterweight to crypto-optimist narratives; informed central bank research on digital currencies.
